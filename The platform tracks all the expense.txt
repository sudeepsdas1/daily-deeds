The platform tracks all the expenses done via the bajaj upi app(as most of the transactions done now-a-day are upi based) and after completion of certain payment it lets the user to categorise the payment into various category like food,shopping ,entertainment investment and add sub-categorise them eg.(Food -> Pizza).This data is stored and is analysed and  visually integrated on the users performance dashboard and on analysis it compares it with the user specified personal goal regarding to his spendings,investment and savings
